Hopes for the return of Bolshunov collapsed

December 19, 2021 by Mark

On December 19, together with Gleb Zetivykh Bolshunov, he won a bronze medal in the team sprint.

The first personal medal of the Russian women's team: Reztsova won bronze in the mass start

December 14, 2021 by Chris

As part of the fourth stage of the Biathlon World Cup in Annecy, a women's 12.5 km mass start took place. Elvira Eberg of Sweden won gold, Julia Simon from France won silver, and Christina Reztsova from Russia won bronze. This is the first personal medal at the World Cup this season for a representative of the Russian women's team. In addition, this is the first personal KM medal in Reztsova's career.

Race results:
  1. Elvira Eberg (Sweden) - gold
  2. Julia Simon (France) - silver
  3. Christina Reztsova (Russia) - bronze
